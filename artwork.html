Visual Artwork



Here is a diverse collection of my physical artwork, showcasing my exploration of various physical mediums such as painting and sculpture. These works are purely visual, reflecting my dedication to creativity and my willingness to experiment with various styles to practice and refine my skills. This approach mirrors my aspiration to become a product designer, where the ability to try new things and refine ideas is crucial. By navigating through different creative processes, I aim to bring a unique perspective to what I create, blending artistic innovation with practical functionality.
